Accessibility statement

The websites fava.be and thomasdevriese.be are committed to being accessible in accordance with EN 301 549 v3.2.1 and the European Accessibility Act (EAA).

This accessibility statement applies to both these sites.

Compliance status

The websites are fully compliant with the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) version 2.2 level AA standard.

Preparation of this statement

Internal accessibility audits were conducted, utilizing: HTML, WAI-ARIA, CSS, JavaScript, VoiceOver and TalkBack.

  • This statement was prepared on 05/05/2023.
  • The statement was last reviewed on 27/09/2024.
